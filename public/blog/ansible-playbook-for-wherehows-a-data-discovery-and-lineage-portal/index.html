Ansible playbook for WhereHows (A Data Discovery and Lineage Portal)

Oct 18, 2016

WhereHows, a data discovery and lineage portal. At LinkedIn, WhereHows integrates with all of their data processing environments and extracts coarse and fine grain metadata from them. Then, it surfaces this information through two interfaces:

  • A web application that enables navigation, search, lineage visualization, annotation, discussion, and community participation

  • An API endpoint that empowers automation of other data processes and applications.

Here I am creating an Ansible playbook to play around with WhereHows.
